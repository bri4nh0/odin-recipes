No-Mayo Egg Salad

Description

For those who don't like egg salad with mayonnaise, there's an alternative for all of you out there. We made sure to compensate for the lack of taste by making sure it is tastier and healthier! You can easily enhoy this on top of a piece of toast or in an entire sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon plain yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons tahini
  • 2 teaspoons za'atar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cornichon pickle (Optional)
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and coarsely chopped

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, tahini, za'atar, salt, and pepper together in a bowl
  2. fold in pickle
  3. Gently stir eggs into tahini mixture
